How do fees work?

Listing fee: It costs US$ 0.20 USD to publish or renew listings. Listing fees are converted into your Payment account currency at the time the listing is published or renewed. Listings are active for four months or until sold.

Transaction fee: When an item sells, we charge a 5% transaction fee on the item and postage prices.

Payment processing fee: When an item sells, a % + payment processing fee is deducted from the sale total.
