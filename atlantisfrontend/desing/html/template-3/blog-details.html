5-Star Buys: A Perky Pressed-Flower Necklace

May 11, 2021 • by Jackie Buddie

In our 5-Star Buys series, we spotlight popular Nookx items that recently struck a chord with shoppers—and let their rave reviews do the talking.

There’s a reason both jewelry and flowers are perennial favorites in the gifting game—so imagine the irresistible appeal of a keepsake that combines the two. Handcrafted by the Montreal-based sister team behind Emery and Opal, this delicate pressed-flower necklace features a miniature posy of pretty petals, carefully preserved in a transparent resin disc. And with six fetching options to pick from—including sunny daisies, ethereal baby’s breath, and dreamy violet—there’s a blossom to suit every style. Available in silver or gold with a bevy of add-on personalization options like initial charms and birthstones, this stunner makes an inspired choice whether you’re looking to treat yourself, show love to a friend, or discover an extra-thoughtful Mother’s Day gift that’s just as unique as she is. Just ask this delighted daughter:

“I bought this as a gift for my mom. She loves purple, so I got her the purple pressed flower with the gold beaded chain and she loves it! It reminded her of when she was younger and she pressed flowers with her grandma. It looks so good!” — BM
